Characteristics of Computer

Class 9 - Chapter Summary

Key Characteristics

  • Speed: Computers can process data and perform calculations at incredibly high speeds.
  • Accuracy: Highly accurate, with low chances of errors if input and instructions are correct.
  • Storage: Large storage capacities to store vast amounts of data.
  • Automation: Computers execute programmed tasks automatically without human intervention.
  • Versatility: Capable of performing a wide range of tasks, from simple to complex.
  • Diligence: Computers work continuously without getting tired, maintaining efficiency.
  • Reliability: They provide consistent results when properly maintained.
